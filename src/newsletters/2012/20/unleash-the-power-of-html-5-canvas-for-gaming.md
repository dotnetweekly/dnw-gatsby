HTML 5 browsers and HTML 5 for Windows 8 Metro are now serious candidates for developing modern games.

With the canvas, you have access to an <strong>hardware accelerated</strong> space where you can draw the content of your game and with some tips and tricks you will be able to achieve a splendid <strong>60 frame per second</strong> render.
